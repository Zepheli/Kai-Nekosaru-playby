Kai Nekosaru

A teenager standing at around 170 centimeters in height usually with a grin on his face always looking for a friendly spar. He has Ash Blond short spikey hair and seems very muscular despite his age. He is usually seen shirtless with a jacket or cloak over his shoulders while wearing light blue pants but is barefoot most of the time. Fire and small explosions can be seen flickering around him wherever he goes.
